Pioneering Blockchain Computing for Science

Younger Wu founded Genyte with a vision to revolutionize scientific computing through blockchain technology. With a background in computational biology and distributed systems, he recognized the critical need for accessible, transparent, and verifiable computing power in biochemistry research.

His work bridges the gap between cutting-edge blockchain technology and the complex computational demands of modern biochemistry, enabling researchers worldwide to tackle problems that were previously beyond reach.
